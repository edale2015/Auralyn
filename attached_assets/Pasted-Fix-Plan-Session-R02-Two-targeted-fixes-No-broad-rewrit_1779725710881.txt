QuestionER trigger if YESIs this the worst headache of your life?Yes → ER (thunderclap / subarachnoid)Any stiff neck or sensitivity to light?Yes → ER (meningitis)Any sudden vision loss or weakness in face/arm?Yes → ER (stroke)Fever over 104°F with confusion?Yes → ER
