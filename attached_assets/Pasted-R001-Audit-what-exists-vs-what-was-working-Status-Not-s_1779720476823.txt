R005 — Fix disposition: rule-based only, no LLM disposition, physician review gate enforced
Status: ⏳ Not started
Files to find first: Disposition logic from R001/R002
The disposition engine must:

Never fire before 4 questions answered — hard stop
Never send ER for nausea + stomach pain alone — those are urgent care
ER triggers (must be confirmed by a direct follow-up question, not assumed):

Worst headache of life (confirmed yes)
Chest pain + shortness of breath (both confirmed)
Blood in vomit (confirmed)
Unable to stay conscious / altered mental status
Signs of stroke (facial droop, arm weakness, speech change)


Every disposition message must include a reason:

ER: "Based on [symptom], this needs emergency evaluation right away."
Urgent care: "Your symptoms sound like something we can help with at urgent care."


Physician review gate: Before sending ANY disposition, set awaitingPhysicianReview = true and hold. The physician queue receives the case summary. Only after physician confirmation (or a 3-minute timeout with urgent care default) does disposition send.

Acceptance criteria:

Nausea + stomach pain → urgent care, NOT ER
"Worst headache of my life" confirmed → ER with explanation
Disposition sent only after ≥4 questions answered
Disposition message always contains "based on" or "because"
Physician review flag is set before disposition sends
