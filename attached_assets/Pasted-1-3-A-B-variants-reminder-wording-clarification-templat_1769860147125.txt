3) Physician “request clarification” templates (copy/paste)

These work with your /api/review/:encounterId/request-clarification.

Template 1 (single missing detail)
Thanks — one quick question so we can advise safely:
1) When did this start?
Reply with a short answer (e.g., “today 2pm”).

Template 2 (severity + red flags)
To guide next steps, please reply:
1) Any trouble breathing, chest pain, fainting, severe weakness/confusion, or severe bleeding? (Yes/No)
2) Rate your worst symptom 0–10.

Template 3 (meds/allergies checkpoint)
Before we advise, please reply with:
1) Any medication allergies?
2) Current medicines (or “none”).
3) Are you pregnant or could you be pregnant? (Yes/No/Not sure)
