Complaint completion criteria

A complaint is ready to mark Sprint 1 complete when:

under-triage is low and explainable

major mismatch patterns are understood

at least 20–50 gold reviews exist

graph heatmap shows no obvious blind spots

reruns are stable

rule suggestions are mostly low-priority
