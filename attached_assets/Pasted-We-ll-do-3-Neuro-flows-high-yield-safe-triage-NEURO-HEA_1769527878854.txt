Syncope / near-syncope
Where it belongs

Usually CARDIO (primary), with overlap in neuro/metabolic.

Add a dedicated system + complaint

System: CARDIO

Flow: CARDIO_SYNCOPE_V1

Chief complaint: SYNCOPE

Why

Syncope triage is cardiac first: chest pain, palpitations, exertional syncope, family history sudden death, abnormal vitals, GI bleed, pregnancy, etc. Neuro questions are secondary.

Chronic pain
Where it belongs

Depends on location, but a practical structure is:

MSK: back pain, neck pain, joint pain

NEURO: headache chronic, neuropathic pain syndromes (sciatica overlaps)

RHEUM (optional later): inflammatory patterns

Neuropathy (numbness/tingling, burning pain)
Where it belongs

NEURO.

Add a dedicated flow

Chief complaint: NEUROPATHY

Why

Needs pattern questions (stocking-glove, unilateral radicular, weakness, diabetes, B12 risk, chemo, alcohol), plus red flags (acute weakness, bladder/bowel).
