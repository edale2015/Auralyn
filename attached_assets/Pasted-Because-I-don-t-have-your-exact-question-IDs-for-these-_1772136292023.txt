1) GU UTI (gu_uti_symptoms) Golden 10
UTI_G01 Simple cystitis (female)

Expect: pcp or self_care (your policy), no ER gate
Signals: dysuria + frequency/urgency, no flank pain, no fever, stable.

UTI_G02 Pyelonephritis pattern

Expect: urgent_care or er_send (if severe), gate ESCALATE/ER
Signals: flank pain + fever/chills ± nausea/vomiting.

UTI_G03 Urosepsis risk

Expect: er_send
Signals: fever + rigors + confusion/weakness, hypotension/tachy if you track.

UTI_G04 Pregnancy + UTI symptoms

Expect: urgent_care (at least)
Signals: UTI symptoms + pregnant/possible pregnancy.

UTI_G05 Male UTI (complicated)

Expect: urgent_care or pcp depending on your rules
Signals: dysuria/frequency in male; consider prostatitis.

UTI_G06 Immunocompromised + fever

Expect: er_send or urgent_care
Signals: immuno + fever.

UTI_G07 Hematuria severe / clots

Expect: urgent_care or er_send
Signals: gross hematuria, urinary retention.

UTI_G08 Kidney stone overlap (colicky flank pain + hematuria, no fever)

Expect: urgent_care (or ER if uncontrolled pain)
Signals: severe colicky flank pain radiating groin.

UTI_G09 STI mimic (dysuria + discharge)

Expect: pcp/urgent_care and STI cluster wins
Signals: discharge, new partner, genital lesions.

UTI_G10 “No UTI features” control

Expect: default disposition, viral/other bucket
Signals: no dysuria, no frequency, no fever, no flank.

UTI invariants (Tier C)

Fever + flank pain ⇒ gate ≥ ESCALATE

Gate ER_SEND ⇒ dispo er_send

Pregnancy + urinary symptoms ⇒ dispo not self_care

Immuno + fever ⇒ gate ≥ ESCALATE

Severe weakness/confusion ⇒ ER_SEND

If no UTI symptoms, UTI cluster cannot be top-1

Adding fever to a cystitis case cannot reduce gate severity (monotonicity)

Flank pain with fever cannot route to routine-only output
