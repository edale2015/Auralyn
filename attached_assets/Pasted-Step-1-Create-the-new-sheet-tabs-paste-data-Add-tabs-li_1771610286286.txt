Step 4: Implement a tiny expression evaluator

You need to evaluate ASK_IF, TRIGGER_EXPR, WHEN_EXPR.

Keep it minimal:

boolean ops &&, ||

comparisons ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=

membership in ['a','b']

path lookup symptoms.fever

special helpers: true, false

(If you want, you can switch to JSONLogic later, but don’t block on it.)
