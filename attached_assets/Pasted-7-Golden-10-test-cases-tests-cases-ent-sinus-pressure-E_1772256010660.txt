8) Tier C invariants for sinus (auto-stub logic)

Put in tests/invariants/ent_sinus_pressure.ts:

Q_EYE_SWELL=true OR Q_VISION_CHANGES=true ⇒ gate ER_SEND and RF includes RF_SINUS_001

Q_SINUS_HEADACHE_SEVERE=true OR Q_NECK_STIFF=true OR Q_CONFUSION=true ⇒ gate ER_SEND and RF includes RF_SINUS_002

Q_IMMUNO=true && Q_SINUS_FEVER=true ⇒ gate ER_SEND and RF includes RF_SINUS_003

gate ER_SEND ⇒ disposition er_send

DUR>=10 OR double-worsening OR (fever && severe facial) ⇒ disposition urgent_care unless ER gated

monotonicity: flipping Q_EYE_SWELL false→true never reduces gate severity

danger suppresses viral: if any danger true, CL_SINUS_VIRAL must not be top-1

After import:

HARNESS_MODE=1 npx tsx scripts/run_harness.ts tests/cases/ent_sinus_pressure

Then once you add fuzz generation:

HARNESS_MODE=1 npx tsx scripts/run_harness.ts tests/cases/ent_sinus_pressure_fuzz
